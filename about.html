Our History

Our story begins back in 1974. When head chef Mark Thomas moved to Parkville, from his home in New York. He quickly learned that there was nowhere to get a slice of pizza in his new town. Knowing that he couldn't live in a town without a pizza spot, he decided to take it upon himself to open up Parkville's first pizzeria. Mark's Pizzeria was a hit and grew rapidly. What started off as a small restaurant quickly became the most popular spot to grab a bite in town. Mark himself is a legend around here, and the residents of Parkville could never imagine the town without Mark's Pizzeria.
